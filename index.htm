Short Bio

I am a tenured Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Mississippi State University. I received my Ph.D. and Master’s in Computer Science from Mississippi State University in 2015 and 2010, respectively. I completed B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Durgapur, India, in 2007. I joined the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Mississippi State University in 2016. Prior to joining Mississippi State, I worked at Northwest Missouri State University for a year as a tenure-track Assistant Professor.

My research is in Software Engineering. Please visit my research page to know about my research interests.
